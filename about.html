Our Mission

Amplifying Voices Through Art

We exist to champion independent creators and bring bold, diverse stories to readers worldwide. Every artist deserves a platform, every story deserves to be told.

InkForge was built on the belief that comics and graphic novels are one of the most powerful mediums for storytelling — blending visual art with narrative depth to create experiences that stay with readers forever.

Our Journey

The InkForge Timeline

From a dream to a movement — here's how we grew.

2018

The Beginning

Founded by a collective of 5 creators in a small Portland studio with a shared vision for indie comics.

2020

First Bestseller

Our debut graphic novel "Shadows of Eternity" hits #1 on independent comic charts and sells over 10,000 copies.

2022

Expansion

Grew to 25+ team members, launched distribution partnerships across North America and Europe.

2024

Award Recognition

Multiple Eisner and Harvey Award nominations. 50+ titles published with a growing readership of 50K+ worldwide.

2026

The Future

Launching our digital platform, expanding into animation, and continuing to champion independent creators worldwide.
